Book Reviews

Percy Jackson: Chalice of the Gods

This book is exactly what you'd expect from a Percy Jackson book! It was a very nostalgic read and I loved every second of it. It was funny, exciting and the romance was adorable.

Good Omens

This book is such a fun read! The character are lovable, especially Crowley and Aziraphale. I love how it has a new take on Christianity and how a demon and angels are friends and love eachother.

Legends & Lattes

This was a very cozy and cute read! It's a very chill book about Viv starting a new life for herself and overcoming challenges. With a cute and subtle romance thrown in the mix.
